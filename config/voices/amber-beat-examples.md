## Key Patterns Observed

1. **Item length**: Real items range 15-35 words (not strictly 21-25). Average is ~25 words.
2. **Data density**: Most items include at least one number. Some (~20%) are data-light event reports.
3. **Sentence structure**: Single sentence, factual, no opinion. Some items are longer compound sentences.
4. **Priority ordering**: Policy/visa news comes first, then demand/enrollment, then university, then PBSA deals.
5. **PBSA development news**: Specific (city, beds, developer, cost, target date) — very common in UK section.
6. **Total items per week**: 25-47 across all regions.
